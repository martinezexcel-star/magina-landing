Magina Operational Architecture Framework

A simple method to stabilize Excel-driven environments and scale them safely.

1) Structural Diagnosis
Identify model issues, table structure gaps, dependency risk, and reliability failures.
2) Model Re-Architecture
Redesign the data model and logic layers for performance, clarity, and maintainability.
3) Controlled Automation
Automate only after architecture is sound — avoid “automation on top of chaos”.
4) Scalable Integration
Integrate SharePoint, Power Automate, Power BI when scale and governance require it.
