How the process usually works

1. We define what your business needs most

That may be a clearer landing, better materials, more professional presentation, more operating structure or a combination of those.

2. We build the core pieces

Depending on the plan, Magina sets up the main assets such as landing pages, digital sales materials, social media content workflows and structured support paths.

3. You request updates through a clearer system

Instead of messy back-and-forth, requests become more organized through a structured path where you can ask for landing updates, social media content and digital sales materials and, when included, use your client portal to keep everything clearer.

4. We improve and expand over time

As the business grows, more content, more materials, more structure and more automation can be added without pretending everything is already fully automatic.

FAQ

A few answers before you decide.

Do you help with legal or tax business setup?

No. Magina does not replace legal, tax or formal registration services. The value is in helping your business look clearer, feel more professional and operate with better structure.

So what does Magina help create?

It helps create a clearer business presence, stronger digital sales materials, better presentation, social media support and more structured ways to request landing updates and ongoing work.

Will this help me sell more?

That is one of the main goals. Better positioning, clearer offers, stronger materials and a more professional client path reduce friction and support sales.

Is the portal the main thing I am buying?

No. The portal is useful because it helps organize requests such as landing updates, social media content and digital material requests, but the main value is helping your business become easier to understand, trust, operate and buy from.

Does everything already happen automatically?

Not completely. The system is being built in a structured way so it can become more automated over time, but today the promise is guided progress with structure, not an instant self-serve experience.

Can I start simple and expand later?

Yes. That is one of the strongest parts of the model. You can start with the layer your business needs now and add more later.
